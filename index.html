A Legacy Carved in Stone and Story

Mankessim stands as the historical capital of the Fante people. Founded by the legendary leaders Obunumankoma, Odapagyan, and Oson, who led their people from Tekyiman to their present home.

Today, Mankessim is more than just a historical monument; it is a thriving hub of commerce, culture, and community. From the sacred Nananompow grove to the bustling central market, every corner of this city whispers tales of resilience and regality.
